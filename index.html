How does the Pomodoro method work?

The Pomodoro method is simple and lasts two hours. First, you perform an activity for 25 minutes. When the time is up, rest for 5 minutes. So on until the two hours are complete. As a reward, you rest for another 30 minutes.


breaks

During your rest time, it's a good idea not to do anything that requires a lot of mental effort. Otherwise, your mind will not be able to reorganize and integrate what you have learned. As a result, you won't be able to give the next Pomodoro your best effort. During this break, you can eat, drink a glass of water, watch television, stretch, access social media or just relax however you see fit.


Advantages of using the Pomodoro technique

  • Relieves anxiety;
  • Increases focus and concentration by reducing interruptions;
  • Increases awareness of decisions;
  • Increases motivation and keeps it constant;
  • Reinforces determination to achieve objectives;
  • Improves the work or study process;
  • Strengthens your determination to continue applying yourself in the face of complex situations.
